I am an Electrical Engineer from the University of Chile, currently I divide my time between my alma mater and the Pontifical Catholic University of Chile, working on Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) to address different medical tasks, such as medical report generation or medical visual question answering (VQA).

Previously, I worked in Computer Vision on different problems, including object detection, people detection in mines to prevent fatalities, action classification, and supermarket shelf detection.

My research interests include multimodality, improving the projection of images into the latent space of Large Language Models, self-supervised learning, computer vision, and reinforcement learning.


Work Experience
======

Reserach Engineer at iHEALTH (Millennium Institute for Intelligent Healthcare Engineering) <br>
November 2023 - Present

- Developed new ML models able to process chest X-rays and write medical reports in a radiologist style. 
- In-context Learning and Finetuning of a Large Multimodal Model for Radiology Report Generation
- Used open-source LLMs (LLaMA, Vicuna, and Mistral) to address the radiology report generation task.
- Shared on HuggingFace my own Large Multimodal Models (LMM) trained on x-rays datasets.

Data Scientist at Zenta Group <br>
December 2020 - November 2023 

- Trained LSTM neural network using PyTorch to forecast the number of items delivered by a distribution center in the next 7 days, allowing the reduction of the not-used workforce by 7% (Cencosud).
- Developed personalization algorithms to deliver deal coupons to more than 10M clients. Consolidating the growth of the platform by 15 % each month. (Cencosud). 
- Developed a Machine Learning model for customer segmentation using K-means, allowing to divide 120,000 customers into 5 clusters, according to the buying frequency and amount of money expended (Cencosud).

Research Engineer at Advanced Mining Technology Center <br>
June 2017 - December 2017

- Researched line extraction algorithms using a LIDAR 2D laser to fit lines to different objects within subterranean mines.
- Researched obstacle detection and avoidance systems using points cloud produced by a LIDAR 2D laser.
